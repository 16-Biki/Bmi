BODY MASS INDEX(BMI) :

is a numerical value derived from an individual's weight and height.
It is a useful screening tool to categorize individuals based on their body weight relative to their height,
which can help identify potential health risks associated with being underweight, overweight, or obese.

How BMI is Calculated :

BMI is calculated using the following formula: BMI= weight(kg) / height (m)2

Steps for Calculation

Measure Weight: Obtain your weight in kilograms (kg).
Measure Height: Measure your height in meters (m) or inches (in).
Apply the Formula: Plug the values into the appropriate formula.

BMI Categories :

The World Health Organization (WHO) classifies BMI into the following categories:
Underweight: BMI < 18.5
Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
Obesity: BMI ≥ 30
